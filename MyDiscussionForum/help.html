Frequently Asked Questions

To create a post, you must be logged in. Simply click the "Create a post" button on the homepage. If you're not already logged in, you can do so by clicking the login button. Access to admin or moderator dashboards requires specific administrative credentials. If you are an admin, you can log in using the credentials provided to you. Regular users cannot access admin controls or see the admin dashboard as these are reserved for users with administrative privileges.

To search for posts, use the search bar available on every page. Simply enter a keyword or topic, and the platform will display relevant posts. Both registered and unregistered users can use this feature to browse content.

To comment on a post, you must first be logged in. Navigate to the post you want to comment on, and you will find a comment box at the bottom of the post. Enter your comment in the provided field and click submit to add your comment to the thread.

User reputations are built through engagement such as posting, commenting, and receiving upvotes from other users. Your reputation score increases with more active participation and positively received content. You can view your reputation score on your profile page.

Registering on the platform allows you to fully engage with the community by creating posts, commenting, and liking or sharing posts. Registered users can also track their own posting history and reputation, participate in community discussions, and receive updates or notifications related to their interests or posts.
